Cori Bargmann

Cori Bargmann in an american neurobiologist, She is most well known for her work and research on genetic and neural circuit mechanisms of behavior. She also has done reasearch on autism and alzheimer's. She was born in virginia and attened Massachuesetts Institute of Technology. she has been awarded the Breakthrough Prize in Life Sciences and Benjamin Franklin Medal in Life Science
