Special Brownie Recipe

Description

These classic fudgy brownies are a chocolate lover's dream come true, offering a perfect blend of rich, deep cocoa flavor and a decadently moist, gooey texture. Each bite reveals a dense, buttery treat with bursts of creamy chocolate from optional chocolate chips, making them irresistibly indulgent. Ideal for satisfying a sweet craving or sharing with friends and family, these brownies are quick and simple to make yet deliver a luxurious taste experience. Whether you enjoy them warm with a scoop of ice cream or at room temperature with a glass of milk, these brownies are a surefire way to elevate any dessert occasion.

  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup (40g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup (65g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup (90g) chocolate chips or chunks (optional)
  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour an 8x8-inch (20x20 cm) baking pan or line it with parchment paper.
  2. Melt the butter: In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter in the microwave or use a double boiler on the stove. Let it cool slightly.
  3. Mix sugar, eggs, and vanilla: Stir the granulated sugar into the melted butter. Mix in the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Add the vanilla extract and stir until combined.
  4. Combine dry ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder.
  5. Add dry ingredients to wet: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips (optional): If using chocolate chips or chunks, gently fold them into the batter.
  7. Pour into pan: Spread the batter evenly in the prepared baking pan.
  8. Bake: Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs. The brownies should look set on the edges but still slightly gooey in the middle.
  9. Cool and cut: Let the brownies cool completely in the pan before cutting into squares.
